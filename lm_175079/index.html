Characteristics of essays

What is an essay? What types of essays are there?
 
An essay is a piece of well-structured academic writing that usually presents the writer's opinion on a certain matter. Essays can cover a variety of topics as well as follow different formats and structures. Different countries also have different expectations of what an essay should be like.
 
There are several different types of essays that you may have to write. The essay type determines the style, structure and organisation and gives hints on the expected content of your essay, which is why it is important to know the different types and the expectations associated with each type.
 
Some of the main essay types include:
  • Argumentative - presenting an argument with the supporting ideas and opposing ideas of an argumentative issue
  • Persuasive - trying to  convince others to agree with our facts, share our values etc.
  • Comparison and contrast - either comparing something or contrasting something
  • Descriptive - painting pictures with words, giving detaild descriptions
  • Narrative - telling a sory about real-life experience
  • etc.
