Rules for essay writing

What are the DO's and DON'Ts of essay writing?

Try the following interactive activity to check your understanding of what the rules of writing good essays are. 
Rules for essay writing
DO's
  • use formal language
  • use sophisticated and academic vocabulary
  • use a clear structure
  • use a variety of word choice
  • use a variety of grammatical structures
  • organise your writing well
  • link your ideas clearly and logically
  • plan before you write
  • time your work carefully
  • leave time to revise, edit and rewrite your work
  • stay on the given topic
DON'Ts
  • use contractions
  • use slang and colloquial expressions
  • be impolite and informal
  • use other people's ideas without referring to the source
  • try to be excessively clever or funny
  • write down everything you can think of
  • include irrelevant information
  • stray from the topic
