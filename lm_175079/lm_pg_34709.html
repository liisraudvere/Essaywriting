Connecting ideas

How can I connect my ideas well? How can I transition from one topic to the next?

In order to write effective texts you need to connect your ideas within a sentence and between sentences. This is where the linking (or cohesive) devices and transitions come into play. A linking device does exactly what the name says - it links clauses into sentences and sentences into paragraphs. A transition helps you to move from one topic to the next, from one sentence to the next.

Some linkers and transitions you are already familiar with (e.g. and, but, then), but following you will find an extensive list of linking devices, categorised by their function, that you can use in all your writing. You can also find a printable/downloadable list below.
